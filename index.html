    About Me

    Back at university, I explored the intersection of computer science and art, crafting everything from intricate app designs to charming video game experiences. Along the way, I interned for a startup, played a key role in launching a new budgeting app, and even immersed myself in design during a semester in Seoul. Now as a recent graduate, I'm eager to apply my skills in design and software engineering.

    As an artist, I strive to construct experiences that are not only functional but also aesthetically pleasing. My background in painting informs my approach, as I aim to make every project a blend of beauty and usability.

    Outside of programming and design, you can find me with a paintbrush in hand, exploring the Lands Between, or hiking up a new trail.
