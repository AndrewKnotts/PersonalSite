Uthrive Internship

During the Summer of 2022, I interned for a startup company called Uthrive. The main focus of the company is to help customers always use the right card for purchases to maximize their credit card rewards. During my internship, I was given various tasks such as redesigning the home screen of the mobile app, creating the UI for a new web extension, fixing problems with the website, and redesigning customer emails.

Home Screen

Problem:

Users don't want to see the same thing every time they open an app. Uthrive's previous home screen contained too little variation in what users saw on subsequent visits. This problem is all the more important in an app which emphasizes savings over time.

During my research, I tried to focus on improving the amount of detail users could see with their savings. I wanted to make it clear to customers how much they were saving and missing in rewards, with an easy to navigate and clean design.

Prototypes

Home screen states based on type of user (first time/free/premium)

Switching between week/month/year, viewing specific data for a day, making transactions window larger.

Web Extension

I created mockups for a new web extension which became the basis for the final design.
